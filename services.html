FAQ

Common booking questions.

How early should we book wedding coverage?

Four to eight weeks is ideal for wedding dates. Larger multi-day bookings should be discussed earlier so team size, drone needs, and deliverables can be planned properly.

Can photography and videography be booked together?

Yes. Photo, video, reels, drone, and cinematic edits can be coordinated under one plan for a smoother event experience.

Do you cover product and corporate work?

Yes. The studio handles product photography, fashion campaigns, catalog shoots, corporate portraits, and workplace brand libraries.
